The FUTBALL CLUB BARCELONA (abbreviated as FCB) coomonly referred as Barcelona also known as Barca is a football team based in the city of Barcelona, Catalonia , Spain, that play in the La Liga Santander. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, Spanish, English, Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture.
Domestically, Barcelona has won a record 74 trophies: 26 La Liga, 30 Copa del Rey, 13 Supercopa de España, 3 Copa Eva Duarte, and 2 Copa de la Liga trophies, as well as being the record holder for the latter four competitions. In international club football, the club has won 20 European and worldwide titles: 5 UEFA Champions League titles, a record 4 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, a joint record 5 UEFA Super Cup, a record 3 Inter-Cities Fairs Cup, and 3 FIFA Club World Cup.
The club has a long-standing rivalry with Real Madrid; matches between the two teams are referred to as El Clásico.According to UEFA club coefficient ranking Barcelona stands 2nd 9 points behind the leaders Real Madrid.
The club is also renowed by its stadium name Camp Nou having capacity of approximately 100000 seats. The moto of the club is Mes que en club meaning it is more than a club.
Today the club is also famous for Lionel Andres Messi who is regarded as one of the greatest in World Football.
